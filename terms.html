1. About our services

We are an independent business providing in-store, in-person repair of device hardware — such as screens, batteries, charging ports and cameras — for phones, tablets, computers, wearables and consoles. We do not provide remote technical support, phone unlocking, jailbreaking, account recovery, bypassing of security locks, or software modification services.

If, after opening a device, we find an additional or different fault, we will stop and contact you with a revised quote for your approval before continuing. Quotes are valid for 14 days unless stated otherwise.
