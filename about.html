About Repair Pro

We do it properly.

Repair Pro was built around a single idea: a repair should outlast the problem. We're not the cheapest shop in town — and we're not trying to be. Our brand is quality.

Quality over a quick, cheap fix.

Anyone can slap a bargain screen on a phone and send you out the door. A few weeks later the touch goes patchy, the colours wash out, or the battery swells. That's not a repair — it's a postponement.

We do it differently. Every repair is carried out in-store by our own technicians using new, OEM-grade parts held to the same standard as the originals. We charge a fair, fixed price for work that's built to last, and we back it for a full twelve months. You pay once, and it stays fixed.

A real shop, real people.

Repair Pro is an independent business with a physical storefront inside Chelsea Market. You can walk in, hand your device across the counter, and watch it get looked at. No mailing it away to a warehouse, no faceless call centre.

To be clear about what we are: we repair device hardware in person. We do not provide remote technical support, phone unlocking, jailbreaking, account recovery, or software modification. We're independent and not affiliated with Apple, Samsung, Google or any other brand.
